Theme: 'Escaping Reality'

Context

A creative interactive story project focused on building branching narratives through HTML linking. The theme explores the concept of escaping reality through different story paths.

Content Overview

  • Interactive narrative game with multiple choice paths
  • Interconnected HTML pages with consistent styling
  • Story centered around a mysterious garden hole discovery
  • Multiple endings ranging from escape to failure scenarios
